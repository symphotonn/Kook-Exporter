## Title
Kook Chat Export

## Summary (132 characters max)
Export chat history from Kook channels to JSON/CSV with images. Simple, fast, and privacy-focused.

## Description

### English

**Export your Kook chat history with one click!**

Kook Chat Export lets you easily backup and export chat messages from any Kook channel. Perfect for archiving important conversations, creating backups, or migrating data.

**Features:**
• Export to JSON or CSV format
• Download images along with messages
• Filter by date range
• Set message limits
• Fast batch downloading
• Privacy-focused - all data stays on your device

**How to use:**
1. Navigate to any Kook channel
2. Click the extension icon
3. Choose your export options
4. Click "Export Chat History"

**Privacy:**
This extension does NOT collect any data. All exports are saved directly to your device. No analytics, no tracking, no data sharing.
